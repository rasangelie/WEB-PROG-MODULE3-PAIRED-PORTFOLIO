Daily Recommended Calorie Tracker

The calorie tracker collects your age, height, weight, biological sex, and level of activity, and tracks your daily food intake. It then displays how much calories you should eat for the remainder of the day.

The height input only accepts centimeters (cm), and the weight input only accepts kilograms (kg).
