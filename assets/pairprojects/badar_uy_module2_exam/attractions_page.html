Explore the different attractions that can be found in Hokkaido, Japan. Hokkaido, the northernmost of Japan’s main islands has many attractions whether it’s cultural, tourist, or ecological attractions that tourist can visit.

Destinations.

目的 地

SAPPORO BEER MUSEUM

This is Japan’s only museum dedicated to beer, which explores the long history of Sapporo Beer.

SUSUKINO, SAPPORO

Japan’s largest entertaining district north of Tokyo. Packed with stores, bars restaurants, karaoke shops, pachinko parlors and red light establishments.

NISEKO, SKI

Japan’s #1 snow resort, located in Hokkaido. Comprised of 4 interlinked ski resorts-Grand Hirafu, Niseko Village, and An’nupuri.

LAKE, MASHU

Located in the Akan Mashu National Park in Eastern Hokkaido, this caldera lake is considered to be the most beautiful lake in Japan.

DAISETSUZAN
NATIONAL PARK

Largest National Park in Japan which features the Daisetsuzan Mountain Range. The range includes Mount Asahi, the highest mountain in Hokkaido.

FURRANO
LAVENDER FIELDS

Lavender starts blooming in late June and reaches its peak from around mid July to early August.
