STUDIO GHIBLI

Studio Ghibli Inc. is a Japanese animation film studio hedquarted in Koganei, Tokyo.
The studio is one of the most highly regarded animation studios in the world , best known for its animated feature films, short films, commercials, and collaborated video games with various game studios. Five of Studio Ghibli’s films are among ten highest-grossing anime feature films made in Japan.

Now, Studio Ghibli is celebrating 20 years of Spirited Away. With this, the Hayao Miyazaki’s
Academy Award-winning masterpiece retuns to cinemas for a limited time in celebration of its 20th Anniversary.




GLOBAL IMPACT

The international success of Studio Gibili production has introduced
many people around the world to the medium of Japanese animation and
has embrace many young kids and even artists.

Studio Gibili's films has been screened at international film festivals
and show creators have utilized their storytelling and animation techniques
in order to give more life to their animations.

Their revolutionary animation and storytelling techniques, coupled with
their precise attention to detail and character development
make Studio Gibili's films engaging and captivating.
